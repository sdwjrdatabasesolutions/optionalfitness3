Our Story

Founded in 2010, SDWJR World Homeless Organization ( Unknown as SDWJR RELIEF) has been at the forefront of the fight against homelessness. What began as a small local initiative has grown into a global movement with operations in over Africa, witin the United States, with local church organizations, food banks and with organizations across the World. .He has worked with organizations that helps the homeless across America.

Our mission is to provide immediate relief through shelter and meals while addressing the root causes of homelessness through education, skill training, job services, and Get up and running initiatives.

We believe everyone deserves a safe place to call home and the support needed to rebuild their lives with dignity.
