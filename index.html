About The Podcast

About TBA News Network TBA News Network is an independent digital news and media platform committed to fearless journalism, cultural truth-telling, and unapologetic accountability. Founded to amplify underrepresented voices, TBA News Network delivers in-depth reporting, sharp political analysis, and real-time commentary on global affairs, Black diaspora issues, social justice, economics, technology, and power structures shaping our world. At the core of TBA News Network is a belief that news should challenge systems, not protect them. We go beyond headlines to examine the forces behind policy decisions, global conflicts, civil rights struggles, and economic inequities—connecting local stories to international impact.

Our coverage spans: Breaking national and international news African and diaspora-focused reporting Politics, civil rights, and activism Culture, media accountability, and technology Economic justice and money mindset awareness Led by Activist SDWJR, TBA News Network blends investigative reporting with grassroots perspective, ensuring stories are told with accuracy, context, and moral clarity. Through written journalism, digital media, and social platforms, we inform, educate, and empower audiences to think critically and engage boldly. TBA News Network is not just reporting the news—we are documenting history as it unfolds.
